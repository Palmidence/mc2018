What is this project?

This project was created to foster creativity by sharing the diverse talents of the Monstercat community, through remixing songs that made it onto the Best of 2018 album. This is a community driven initiative where you're encouraged to take your talents to the next level and show us a taste of what you've got!

Will this be a yearly tradition?

Depending on how well it goes, this could be a yearly tradition. However, this is mainly a project with commemorative intent as 2018 was such a definitive year for the label. Additionally, this is an ambitious project that requires a lot of planning and organization.

How do I participate?

In order to participate, you must join the Discord and post in #commands with the name of the track that you are remixing. Only songs from Best of 2018 are eligible to be remixed. You must have a SoundCloud account.
